The purpose of our Movie Ticket Booking Web Application is to provide a convenient, user-friendly platform for movie enthusiasts to browse and book tickets for their favorite films online. By leveraging this app, users can effortlessly explore movie schedules, select preferred showtimes and seats, and securely book tickets from the comfort of their own homes. This application aims to streamline the ticket booking process, reduce wait times, and enhance the overall cinematic experience for users. With features such as user registration, movie browsing, and secure payment processing, our web app is designed to make movie-going more accessible and enjoyable for audiences everywhere.
